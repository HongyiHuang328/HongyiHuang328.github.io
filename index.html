About

Currently, I am a postgraduate researcher (PhD student) at the University of Bristol (UoB) in the UK supervised by Professor Tim Burness and supported by China Scholarship Council.

From 2016 to 2020, I was an undergraduate at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in China under the supervision of Professor Cai Heng Li.

I currently co-organise the Group Theory Seminar based at SUSTech. If you are a young group theorist and want to give a talk, feel free to drop me an email.
