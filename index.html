About

Currently, I am an LMS Early Career Research Fellow hosted by Professor Colva Roney-Dougal at the University of St Andrews in the UK, with financial support from the London Mathematical Society and the Heilbronn Institute for Mathematical Research.

I am also a postgraduate researcher (PhD student) at the University of Bristol (UoB) in the UK supervised by Professor Tim Burness and supported by China Scholarship Council.

From 2016 to 2020, I was an undergraduate at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in China under the supervision of Professor Cai Heng Li.

I am co-organising the 26th Postgraduate Group Theory Conference (PGTC 2025), which will be held in July 2025 at UoB.

The name "Hong Yi Huang" is only used for papers and research profiles (to distinguish myself from millions of "H. Huang"). Please call me "Hongyi" in any other case.
